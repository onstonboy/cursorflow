# Design Style: Botanical / Organic Serif

## Design Philosophy

Soft, earthy, elegant design inspired by nature. Features organic shapes, generous rounded corners, paper grain texture, muted earth tones, and sophisticated serif typography that breathes warmth and natural luxury.

**Vibe**: Earthy, Elegant, Natural, Warm, Serif-led, Organic, Refined, Sustainable

## Layout Ideas

### Hero
Split two-column layout with massive serif typography on left and arched image (200px top radius) on right. Decorative botanical icons float in corners. Quote overlay on image.

### Stats
Horizontal band with subtle sage background and border. Large serif numbers paired with uppercase sans-serif labels in grid.

### Features
Three-column grid with staggered vertical offsets (translate-y-12 on middle card). Soft rounded cards with botanical icons in muted circles.

### How It Works
Dark forest green background with three-step grid. Dotted SVG connecting path. Steps in translucent cards with numbered terracotta badges.

### Benefits
Cards with organic shapes and botanical motifs. Muted earth-tone backgrounds.

### Testimonials
Serif quotes with circular or organic-frame avatars. Soft shadows and rounded corners.

### Pricing
Rounded cards with botanical accent (leaf or stem). Terracotta or sage accent for highlighted tier.

## Design Token System

### Colors
- **Background**: Off-white, cream, or very light sage (#F5F5F0, #E8EDE6)
- **Foreground**: Dark green or brown (#2D3A2D, #3D2C1E)
- **Accent**: Terracotta (#C27B66), Sage (#7A9B76), muted green (#5D7052)
- **Muted**: Sage backgrounds, light terracotta for badges

### Typography
- **Display/Headlines**: Serif (e.g. Cormorant Garamond, Lora, or similar book serif)
- **Body**: Serif or soft sans. Generous line-height.
- **Labels**: Uppercase, sans-serif, letter-spacing

### Border Radius
- Generous: 1rem–2rem on cards and images. Arched top on hero image (e.g. 200px radius).

### Texture
- **Paper grain**: Subtle overlay for warmth. Low opacity.
- **Organic shapes**: Blob-like backgrounds, irregular curves.

### Shadows
- Soft, diffuse shadows. No hard edges. Slight lift on cards.

## Component Patterns

### Buttons
- Rounded (pill or large radius). Earth-tone fill or outline. Hover: slight darken or scale.

### Cards
- White or very light background. Rounded corners. Botanical icon in muted circle. Optional staggered layout (middle card offset down).

### Images
- Arched top edge on hero. Rounded corners. Optional soft border or shadow.

## Iconography
- Botanical: leaves, stems, seeds, flowers. Muted colors. Used in circles or as section accents.
