# Design Style: Organic / Natural

## Design Philosophy

Earth-inspired palette with moss greens, terracotta, and sand tones. Features organic blob shapes, grain texture overlays, asymmetric rounded corners, and soft shadows. Embraces wabi-sabi philosophy with warmth and natural imperfection.

**Vibe**: Earthy, Wabi-sabi, Warm, Natural, Soft, Imperfect, Calm, Organic

## Layout Ideas

### Hero
Centered layout with overlapping organic blob backgrounds. Large serif typography with generous spacing. Trust badge with rounded pill shape. Dual CTAs in pill-shaped buttons.

### Stats
Horizontal grid (2 cols mobile, 4 cols desktop) with centered text. Large serif numbers with hover scale animation. Subtle border separators.

### Product Detail
Two-column split with rotated image frame and organic blob accent. Image has thick white border and soft shadow. Text constrained to max-w-2xl for readability.

### Features
3-column responsive grid with cards featuring varied organic border radii. Blob or leaf-like accents. Soft shadows.

### How It Works
Steps with organic connectors (curved or dotted). Rounded cards. Natural color accents.

### Benefits
Cards with organic shapes. Moss or terracotta accents. Generous padding.

### Testimonials
Serif quotes, rounded avatars, soft shadows. Warm, approachable tone.

### Pricing
Rounded cards with organic blob or sand-toned accents. Highlighted tier with subtle background or border.

## Design Token System

### Colors
- **Background**: Sand, cream, or light warm gray (#F5F0EB, #EDE8E3)
- **Foreground**: Dark moss or brown (#2C3A2C, #4A3728)
- **Accent**: Moss green (#5D7052), Terracotta (#C27B66), Sand (#C4A77D)
- **Muted**: Light terracotta, sage, sand for backgrounds and borders

### Typography
- **Display**: Serif (warm, book-like)
- **Body**: Serif or soft sans. Comfortable reading measure.

### Border Radius
- Asymmetric and varied: use different radii per element (e.g. 1.5rem, 2rem, 2.5rem). Avoid uniform sharp or perfect circles everywhere.

### Shapes
- **Organic blobs**: SVG or CSS blob shapes as backgrounds or accents. Overlapping, soft edges.
- **Grain**: Subtle noise or paper texture overlay.

### Shadows
- Soft, diffuse. Multiple layers for depth. No hard cutoffs.

## Component Patterns

### Buttons
- Pill-shaped or very rounded. Earth-tone fill. Soft shadow. Hover: slight lift or color shift.

### Cards
- White or light background. Varied organic border radius. Thick white border or soft shadow. Optional small rotation (-1deg to 1deg).

### Images
- Rounded corners (organic, not uniform). Thick white border. Soft shadow. Optional slight rotation.

## Anti-Patterns
- No cold or tech-heavy palette
- No perfect grids without organic offset
- No hard geometric shapes as primary motif
- Avoid sterile or corporate feel
